Plays By Ranking

This table was suggested by vader78. This table has one cell for each game in the BGG rankings. If you have played that game, the cell is coloured according to the number of plays you have recorded - white for zero, red for 1, progressing through yellow and green to dark green for 25+. Most cells have tooltips telling you what the game at that rank is. There can be anomalies as the download process can observe different games at the same rank at different times - the BGG rankings may differ from the data used to calculate this table.

There's a tooltip on the row header summarising your feelings on the games. Due to the observation errors mentioned above, some rows won't have exactly 100 games.
